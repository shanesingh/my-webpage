About Me

A little bit about who I am

Hey! I'm Shane Singh — a self-taught developer in the making, based wherever the road takes me. I started learning to code because I wanted to build things that actually matter to me, and I haven't looked back since.

When I'm not staring at a screen, you'll find me on the golf course chasing that elusive birdie, wrenching on cars in the garage, or planning the next trip somewhere I've never been.

This site is my digital garden — a living, growing space where I document my journey, share projects, and connect with people who are into the same things.

Hobbies

The things that keep me sane

Golf is my meditation. There's nothing quite like an early morning round — dew on the fairway, no one else around, just you and the course. I'm a mid-handicapper working on my short game and trying not to lose too many balls in the water.

Cars are in my blood. I grew up watching my dad work on engines, and now I do the same. I love everything from weekend track days to diagnosing a weird knock at 3am. Current project: a BMW E46 that's slowly becoming something special.

Travel is how I reset. I try to go somewhere new at least once a year — ideally somewhere that challenges me, feeds my curiosity, and gives me great photos. Japan, Portugal, and Iceland are recent highlights. Next up: Southeast Asia.
